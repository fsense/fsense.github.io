# Florian Sense

I'm a researcher working at the intersection of computational cognitive science, applied machine learning, and language assessment. My work focuses on how people learn—particularly how they acquire complex skills like a second language—and how to build systems that support learning at scale.

Most of my day-to-day is applied: making curricula machine readable and operationalizable, building NLP and LLM pipelines, developing models of learning and forgetting, and turning research into tools that people actually use. I care about doing this work in a way that's both methodologically serious and operationally practical.

I'm currently contracting as a senior data scientist through [Infinite Tactics](https://infinitetactics.com/www/), working with Tiffany Myers's team at the [Air Force Research Laboratory](https://www.wpafb.af.mil/afrl/711HPW/) on computerized, adaptive learning for foreign language training. I co-supervise [Thomas Wilschut](https://www.thomaswilschut.com/)'s PhD at the University of Groningen together with [Hedderik van Rijn](https://van-rijn.org/), and previously co-supervised [Maarten van der Velde](https://www.maartenvandervelde.com/)'s PhD (with Hedderik and [Jelmer Borst](https://www.jelmerborst.nl/)). I'm open to selected research collaborations and consulting engagements. See [what I do](/what-i-do/) for details.

Outside of that, I maintain side projects in language learning ([Picolingo](https://www.picolingo.app/)) and civic technology ([PaZuFa](https://www.pazufa.de)).

## What I work on

- [**Applied NLP for language assessment**](/what-i-do/#nlp-assessment). Proficiency-level classification, readability modeling, automated scoring
- [**LLM infrastructure and content generation**](/what-i-do/#llm-infrastructure). Structured-output pipelines, evaluation frameworks, production deployment
- [**Computational models of learning and memory**](/what-i-do/#learning-models). Knowledge tracing, forgetting curves, adaptive scheduling
- [**Curriculum and assessment design**](/what-i-do/#curriculum-design). Can-do indicator taxonomies, ILR/CEFR/ACTFL alignment, measurement frameworks
